Alina's Favorite Movie Trailers

Frida

A biography of artist Frida Kahlo, who channeled the painof a crippling injury and her tempestuous marriage into her work.

Forrest Gump

Forrest Gump, while not intelligent, has accidentally been presentat many historic moments, but his true love, Jenny Curran, eludes him.

The Shawhank Redemption

Two imprisoned men bond over a number of years, finding solaceand eventual redemption through acts of common decency.

Pulp Fiction

The film connects the intersecting storylines of Los Angeles mobstersfringe players, small-time criminals, and a mysterious briefcase.

Memento

A man juggles searching for his wife's murderer and keeping hisshort-term memory loss from being an obstacle.

Eyes Wide Shut

A New York City doctor, who is married to an art curator,pushes himself on a harrowing and dangerous night-longodyssey of sexual and moral discovery after his wife admitsthat she once almost cheated on him.

The Pianist

A Polish Jewish musician struggles to survive thedestruction of the Warsaw ghetto of World War II.

Volver

After her death, a mother returns to her home town inorder to fix the situations she couldn't resolve during her life.

Eternal Sunshine of The Spotless Mind

When their relationship turns sour, a couple undergoesa procedure to have each other erased from their memories.But it is only through the process of loss that theydiscover what they had to begin with.

Secret Life of Pets

The quiet life of a terrier named Max is upended whenhis owner takes in Duke, a stray whom Max instantly dislikes.
